About the app

The Meme Generator app is the place where you can select a picture from the gallery and than add some text with different fonts and sizes and colors, and place it in the picture.

You can move the text and place it wherever you want on the picture. You can add up to 3 text line per picture.

After you finish editing the text on the picture you can download it with the text. It will be saved on your computer.
